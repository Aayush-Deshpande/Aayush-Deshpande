# 💫 About Me:
# Hey, I'm Aayush 👋⚡<br><br>Third year CSBS student at JSPM's Rajarshi Shahu College of Engineering, Tathawade, Pune.<br><br>My work hasn't stayed inside a classroom, it's moved through a research lab at an IIT and a space research centre.<br><br>Building toward Forward Deployed Engineer and AI/ML engineering roles, where research doesn't stay on paper, it ships.<br><br>## 🧪 Where I've Actually Worked<br><br>**BSERC, Bharat Space Educational Research Centre** 🛰️ — Currently interning as a Def-Space Tech Intern, building a defense AI dashboard powered by anomaly detection and risk scoring. Real stakes, real systems, no toy datasets.<br><br>**IIT Ropar, VLED Lab** 🔬 — Summer Intern, led federated learning research as team lead, working on privacy-preserving ML at a research institute where the bar for rigor is set brutally high.<br><br>These aren't line items. They're where I learned what "production grade" actually means when the people reviewing your work do this for a living.<br><br>## 🛰️ What That Work Turned Into<br><br>**FedNeMo** 🧠 — a federated fine-tuning framework running on a custom FedRand mechanism, adaptive 2-bit quantization, and entropy-aware aggregation across Nemotron models.<br><br>**PRISM** 🌕 — a lunar rover RL simulation built on real Chandrayaan-2 DFSAR and OHRC data, complete with ice detection, a physics-accurate terrain digital twin, and a PPO agent navigating it with Dijkstra/A* warm starts.<br><br>**Leon** ⚖️ — a legal RAG agent with hybrid dense and BM25 retrieval, where self-critique lives inside the agent's own decision loop instead of being bolted on afterward.<br><br>**AutoML** ⚙️ — an end-to-end pipeline that takes in any dataset and runs the entire ML workflow on its own: preprocessing, feature extraction and selection, model training across multiple algorithms, metric comparison, and picking the best performer. Where there's room to push a model further, it handles hyperparameter tuning too. Feed it data, walk away, come back to a leaderboard.<br><br>## 🎹 Off Duty<br><br>Learning **Japanese** 🇯🇵 and playing **piano** 🎹, two disciplines that punish impatience exactly like debugging a broken training loop does. One's built on pattern and rhythm, the other on pattern and memory. I like the overlap.<br><br>## 📡 Find Me<br><br>Writing about AI/ML on LinkedIn, usually right after a model finally stops throwing NaNs.<br><br>---<br><br>*Building things that are worth building, not just things that look good on paper.* ✨
